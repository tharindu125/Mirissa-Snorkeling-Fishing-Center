About Sea Turtles

Discover the fascinating world of sea turtles as you snorkel through the crystal-clear waters of Mirissa! These gentle marine creatures have been swimming in our oceans for over 100 million years, making them one of the oldest living species on Earth.

Sea turtles are known for their incredible migratory journeys, often traveling thousands of miles between feeding grounds and nesting beaches. They play a crucial role in maintaining the health of marine ecosystems by grazing on seagrass and controlling jellyfish populations.

In Mirissa, you‚Äôll find several species of sea turtles, including the Green Turtle, Hawksbill Turtle, and Olive Ridley Turtle. Watching them glide gracefully through the reefs is a truly magical experience you‚Äôll never forget.
